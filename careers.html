Career Opportunities

Executive Leadership

Chief Scientific Officer (CSO)

Background: Computational biophysics/chemistry with molecular modeling research (Python MD simulations, protein folding preprint)

Focus: Coordinates scientific leads, ensures computational accuracy, collaborates with academic mentors

Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Background: CS/bioinformatics with open-source tool development (Flask apps, GitHub contributions)

Focus: Manages software teams, cloud infrastructure, model integration

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Background: Remote team management (virtual events, Agile project leadership)

Focus: Global workflow optimization, deadline tracking, resource allocation

Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)

Background: SaaS startup experience or business competition wins

Focus: Virtual client acquisition, demo presentations, contract strategy

Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Background: Financial management (student budgets, crowdfunding)

Focus: Grant applications, burn rate analysis, contributor stipends

Scientific Leadership

Physics Lead

Background: Molecular dynamics/quantum mechanics research (LAMMPS/GROMACS projects)

Tools: Python, MATLAB, VMD

Biology Lead

Neuroscience Lead

Chemistry Lead
